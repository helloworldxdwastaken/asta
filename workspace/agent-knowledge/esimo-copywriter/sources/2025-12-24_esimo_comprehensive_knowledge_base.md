# eSIMo - Comprehensive Knowledge Base

## Overview

**eSIMo** is a mobile application that provides international travelers with eSIM technology for seamless connectivity worldwide. The app eliminates the need for physical SIM cards by offering digital eSIMs that can be activated instantly on compatible devices.

## What eSIMo Does (Main Purpose)

**Core Function:**
eSIMo provides travelers with affordable global data plans and international calling capabilities through eSIM technology. The app serves as a one-stop solution for staying connected while traveling abroad without the hassle of physical SIM card swapping.

**Primary Benefits:**
- Avoid expensive roaming fees
- Keep original phone number active while using local data
- Instant activation without physical SIM cards
- Access to 400+ cellular networks worldwide
- 24/7 customer support
